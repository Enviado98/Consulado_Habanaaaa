The Story

From
Factory
to Icon

Capitolium, Brescia, Italy

He stands for everything that gives us hope: he is young, has a sense of humour, a warm heart and a clear point of view. Khaby is living proof that destiny is something that can be challenged.

Khaby came to Turin with his parents when he was just one year old, leaving Senegal under difficult circumstances. During the pandemic, he lost his job and moved back to his family in Chivasso.

In March 2020, he uploaded his first TikTok videos — something nice, something that makes people smile.

The rest is TikTok history. His comedic talent, subtle perspective and open hands are his trademarks. Above all, he is magical and adorable.
